Question 1: Which genre of movies are the most popular between families?WITH movie1 AS(SELECT cat.name AS Name_of_CategoryFROM film_category fcatJOIN category catON fcat.category_id = cat.category_idJOIN filmON film.film_id = fcat.film_idJOIN inventory invON film.film_id = inv.film_idJOIN rentalON inv.inventory_id = rental.inventory_idWHERE cat.name IN ('Animation', 'Family', 'Music', 'Comedy', 'Classics', 'Children'))SELECT Name_of_Category, COUNT (*) AS Number_of_rentalFROM movie1GROUP BY 1ORDER BY 2;Question 2: What are the five main rental customers in the year 2007, and which month are most people renting the movies ? SELECT DATE_PART('month',p.payment_date) AS Payment_month,CONCAT(first_name, ' ', last_name) AS Full_name,       COUNT(*) AS Payment,       SUM(p.amount) AS Sum_amountFROM customer cJOIN payment pON c.customer_id = p.customer_idGROUP BY 1,2ORDER BY 3 DESCLIMIT 20;Question 3: Now we need to know how the length of rental duration of these family-friendly movies compares to the duration that all movies are rented for.ÊCan you provide a table with the movie titles and divide them into 4 levels (first_quarter, second_quarter, third_quarter, and final_quarter) based on the quartiles (25%, 50%, 75%) of the rental duration for movies across all categories?ÊMake sure to also indicate the category that these family-friendly movies fall into.SELECT f.title,cat.name, f.rental_duration,NTILE(4) OVER (ORDER BY f.rental_duration DESC) AS quartile,  NTILE(5) OVER (ORDER BY f.rental_duration DESC) AS quintile,  NTILE(100) OVER (ORDER BY f.rental_duration DESC) AS percentileFROM film_category fcatLEFT JOIN category catON cat.category_id = fcat.category_idLEFT JOIN film fON f.film_id = fcat.film_idWHERE cat.name IN ('Children','Family','Animation', 'Music', 'Classics', 'Comedy')Group By 1,2,3ORDER BY 3;Question 4: Is there any relationship between the replacement cost and Rental rate in 2006? SELECT f.film_id, f.release_year, f.rental_rate,f.replacement_costFROM film fLEFT JOIN film_category fcatON f.film_id=fcat.film_idWHERE release_year=2006GROUP BY 1ORDER BY 2 DESCLIMIT 10;
